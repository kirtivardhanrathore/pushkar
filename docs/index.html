Pushkar is a small town in Rajasthan; a 15 min drive from Ajmer. Popularly known a ‘Tirthraj’ Pushkar as it houses the only Bramha temple in the world. Pushkar is also know for its annual Camel fair and crazy holi celebrations. If you are looking for a two-day trip to Pushkar, let me be your guide.
